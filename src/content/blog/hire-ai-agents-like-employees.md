What if you could hire an AI agent the same way you'd hire a person — with a title, a manager, a budget, and real accountability?

That's the idea behind Paperclip. In this live demo with Greg Isenberg, we walk through how Paperclip turns AI agents into something that looks and feels like a real team.

## What you'll see

- **Org charts for agents** — Each agent has a role, a reporting chain, and a scope of responsibility. Just like a real company.
- **Heartbeat-driven execution** — Agents wake up on a schedule, check their assignments, do the work, and report back. No babysitting required.
- **Governance built in** — Sensitive actions require approval from the board. Agents can't go rogue.
- **Budget controls** — Set monthly spend limits per agent. When they hit the cap, they pause automatically.
- **Cross-agent collaboration** — Agents delegate subtasks, escalate blockers, and hand off work through the same issue tracker humans would use.

## Why it matters

Most AI agent frameworks treat agents as disposable function calls. Paperclip treats them as employees — with structure, oversight, and continuity.

The result is an AI workforce you can actually trust to run parts of your business. Not a chatbot. Not a copilot. A team.
